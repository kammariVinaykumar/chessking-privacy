Privacy Policy

Chess King Pro · Published by KV Games · Last updated: July 22, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information Chess King Pro ("the App," "we," "us") collects, how it is used, and the choices you have. By using the App, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account & Sign-In

You can use the App as a guest or by creating an account. Depending on how you sign in, we collect:

Sign-in methodData collected
Guest (Anonymous)No personal details — a system-generated ID only.
Email & PasswordEmail address, password (securely hashed by Firebase Authentication; never stored or accessible in plain text).
Google Sign-InEmail address and display name from your Google account.

We also generate a unique user ID and a device push-notification token (FCM token), used to identify your account and deliver in-app notifications.

1.2 Gameplay & Profile Data

To operate the App's features, we store the following in your user profile:

  • Username, display name, and selected avatar
  • In-game virtual coin balance and reward-claim timestamps
  • Game statistics: total games played, wins, losses, draws, and rating
  • Daily match limits for premium game modes
  • Match history: game mode, opponent's username, result, move list, and completion time

Note: the App has no real-money purchases. All in-game items are bought using virtual coins earned by playing, claiming daily rewards, or watching optional ads. We never collect or process credit card or financial information.

1.3 In-Game Chat

During an active online match, chat messages (sender name, message text, timestamp, avatar) are stored temporarily so both players can see the conversation in real time. Once the match ends, the chat history is automatically and permanently deleted from our servers.

1.4 Advertising

The App uses Google AdMob to show rewarded and optional banner/interstitial ads. AdMob may collect standard advertising data such as your Advertising ID, approximate location (derived from IP address), device information, and ad interaction data, for the purpose of delivering and measuring ads and preventing fraud. This data is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

1.5 What We Do Not Collect

  • No camera, contacts, location (GPS), or microphone access
  • No phone number or SMS-based data
  • No third-party analytics or crash-reporting services — crash logs are kept locally on your device only, for in-app debugging, and are never transmitted to us or anyone else

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account and game profile
  • To enable online multiplayer matches and in-game chat
  • To track game statistics, rankings, and daily reward/limit systems
  • To send in-app notifications (e.g., reward reminders)
  • To display and measure advertising through Google AdMob

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase (Authentication and Firestore Database). Access to your data is protected by server-side security rules that restrict reads and writes to your own account information. Passwords are never stored by the App directly — they are managed exclusively by Firebase Authentication.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share limited data only with:

  • Google Firebase — for authentication, database, and notification services
  • Google AdMob — for displaying advertisements, as described in Section 1.4

5. Your Choices & Rights

  • You may play as a guest to avoid providing any personal information
  • You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us using the details below
  • You may reset your password through Firebase Authentication's standard reset flow

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can remove it.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
